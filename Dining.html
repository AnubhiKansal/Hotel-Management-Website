 Buffet


Bring your own favorite people or meet new ones at our community table. LYKKE's menu is a discovery in the Nordic food traditions and future. In classics and in experiments. Often with the roots of fish, seafood and meat from the wild, free nature and carefully selected primers from local producers. Our experienced sommeliers will be happy to guide you through the generous wine and drink menu.  

Timings:Monday to sunday 6:pm tp 10pm .

 Brunch

 

Weekends are for brunch. Ever Saturday and Sunday from 12:00 we show up a delicious Nordic brunch buffet with something for everyone. For tones of fine music, you can choose from among other things, puff pastry topped with olives and roasted peppers, boeuf bourguignon, grilled chicken thighs, broccoli pie with blue cheese and tomato, skagen stir, cooked crab claws and several carefully selected dishes created by the kitchen.
                                        
 

 







 Celler


At four seasons, we know a thing or two about  wine. In fact, we know quite a lot. We love wine, which is why we've tucked the precious treasures of a wine connoisseur safely away in our wine cellar. But we love to share, so come on down to the lobby, chat to one of our experienced staff members, and we will help you select the right bottle for your refined tastes.

Timings:Monday to sunday 6:pm tp 10pm .
